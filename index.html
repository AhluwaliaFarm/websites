About Us

Ahluwalia Farm is a family-run farm nestled in Amritsar, Punjab, dedicated to bringing you the purest honey and freshest cage-free eggs. For generations, we have tended our land with respect for nature — nurturing wildflowers for our bees and providing open spaces for our hens.

Our multiflora honey is harvested raw and unprocessed, preserving every drop of natural goodness from diverse Punjab wildflowers. We never blend, heat, or dilute — what you taste is exactly what our bees create.

Alongside our honey, we raise cage-free hens with care and compassion. We believe good food starts with good farming, and we are proud to share that tradition with families across the region.
